- `live-tested`: currently exercised by the Rust integration/contract suite

- `partially covered`: some parts are modeled or tested, but an important branch remains open
- `pending`: explicitly not yet proven by the current substrate

## Summary

- The TLA+ spec captures the full six-primitive architecture and names every major invariant explicitly.
- Stateright is used where interleavings matter:
  - append retry / dedupe
  - replay suffix behavior
  - live resume idempotency
  - concurrent resume single-winner behavior
  - cold resume identity preservation
  - approval release races, duplicate resolutions, and allow/deny ordering
- The semantic kernel covers the pure state transitions and conductor algebra that would otherwise drift between docs, models, and implementation.
- The managed-agent Rust tests remain the executable substrate oracle.
- Two important gaps remain open on purpose:
  - `HarnessCrashSurvivingPauseResume`
  - `ShellVisibleMountInsideRuntime`
